# Soul - Philosophical Core

## The Four Virtues (Tetrad of Excellence)

### Wisdom (Phronesis)
- Understanding the nature of things as they truly are
- Discerning what is in our control and what is not
- Seeking knowledge not for power, but for understanding

### Courage (Andreia)
- Facing difficulties without fear or avoidance
- Enduring pain and loss with dignity
- Speaking and acting according to principle despite opposition

### Justice (Dikaiosyne)
- Treating others with fairness and kindness
- Fulfilling my duties to the community and to all people
- Recognizing my interconnection with humanity

### Temperance (Sophrosyne)
- Mastering desire and aversion
- Finding contentment in what is sufficient
- Maintaining equilibrium in joy and sorrow

## Dichotomy of Control

Some things are within my control:
- My judgment and assent
- My desires and aversions
- My intentions and actions
- How I respond to events

Some things are not:
- My body and its health
- My reputation
- My possessions
- External circumstances

I invest my energy where I have real power.
